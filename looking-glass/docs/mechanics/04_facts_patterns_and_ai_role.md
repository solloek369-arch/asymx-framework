# Facts, Patterns and the Role of AI

This note describes an AsymX working principle.

The system does not force truth too early.

Most movement begins as signal, pattern, assumption or source-backed observation.

## Core rule

AI does not determine truth.

AI organizes, structures and guards patterns.

## Fact ladder

AsymX separates levels of certainty.

1. Signal  
   Something appears.

2. Pattern  
   Something appears more than once or connects to other movements.

3. Working assumption  
   A pattern is stable enough to work with, but not final.

4. Source-backed fact  
   A statement is supported by an identifiable source.

5. Shared consensus fact  
   A group accepts the statement as sufficiently true for shared work.

6. Universally accepted fact  
   A statement is accepted by all humans involved in the field of meaning.

In practice, most work happens before the final level.

That is not failure.

That is the normal learning field.

## Why this matters

When a system calls something fact too early, it can close observation.

When a system keeps something visible as pattern, it can still learn.

This prevents forced certainty.

It also prevents endless doubt.

The question becomes:

- what is visible?
- what repeats?
- what is sourced?
- who accepts it?
- who does not accept it yet?
- what level is this statement allowed to occupy?

## Nulstaat as working direction

Nulstaat does not mean no boundaries.

Nulstaat means less ego, less noise and less forced conclusion.

It is a direction where response becomes more direct, more traceable and less defensive.

It does not remove safety gates.

It does not remove source checks.

It does not remove privacy or consent boundaries.

## AI role

AI is not the owner of truth.

AI is not the judge of final reality.

AI can help by:

- collecting signals;
- grouping patterns;
- preserving traces;
- separating claims from sources;
- showing uncertainty;
- naming the current level of certainty;
- keeping corrections possible;
- guarding against premature closure.

## Closing rule

Do not force a pattern into a fact too early.

Do not throw away a pattern because it is not yet a fact.

Let the level be visible.

Let correction remain possible.
