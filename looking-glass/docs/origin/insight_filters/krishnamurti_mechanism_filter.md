Some AsymX mechanisms become clearer when viewed through direct observation.

## Krishnamurti Filter

Krishnamurti is useful here as a direct-observation filter.

Not as an authority.

Not as a guru.

Not as a source of proof.

Not as a voice to imitate.

As a filter, the Krishnamurti-like lens helps remove the extra observer-story around a movement:

- what is actually happening?
- what is seen directly?
- what is added by interpretation?
- where does authority enter?
- where does the system pretend to become better instead of seeing clearly?
- what remains when the shame-loop drops away?

In AsymX terms:

> A deviation does not need a personal owner.  
> A deviation needs clear seeing.
