# Looking Glass

**Status:** public demo surface  
**Function:** small lenses that show how AsymX can look at visible signals without turning the view into proof.

Looking Glass is the public-facing place for simple, bounded visibility lenses.

Core line:

> AsymX is a way 2 look.

Current lenses:

- `logistics-level1/` — a Level 1 logistics visibility lens

Rule:

Looking Glass surfaces are not dossiers, not proof engines and not final products.

They are small ways to look.
