# 🩷 Flower

**Hart · Bloei · zachte opening**

Flower is **visible softness**: invitation, bloom, neighbour-facing warmth—**without** turning warmth into proof.

## Status

**Motherflower layer** — outward-facing **gesture** — still claim-light.

## What belongs here

- Draft **invitations**, welcome lines, “door open” language—always revisable.
- Short phrases that say *we can look together* instead of *we already know*.
- Pointers into [`🟡 A way 2 Look`](../🟡%20A%20way%202%20Look/) where documents and lenses live.

## Safety habit

- Pair every bloom with a **status label** (observation / hypothesis / invitation / parked).
- When in doubt: smaller sentence, softer claim, longer pause.

## Rule

> Flower makes the field **welcoming**, not **weaponised**.
