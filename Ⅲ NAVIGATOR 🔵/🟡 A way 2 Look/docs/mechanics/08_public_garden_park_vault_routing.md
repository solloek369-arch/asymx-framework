# Public Garden / Park / Vault Routing

## Purpose

AsymX separates **where material lives** while work moves from raw signal toward reusable structure. Not everything valuable belongs in public immediately. Routing protects source material and reduces accidental exposure, while still letting **processed mechanisms** become inspectable in the open.

This note defines a **reusable routing mechanism**: labels for three zones and rules for how material may move between them. It is not a legal policy and not a guarantee against mistakes—it is a discipline for sorting and publishing.

---

## The Three Routing Zones

### Public Garden

Material in the **public garden** is:

- **Processed** — distilled into patterns, guards, or navigation that can be read without importing private backstory.
- **Claim-safe** — wording avoids overstating evidence; uncertainty and limits stay visible.
- **Source-protected** — underlying raw records are not pasted through; origins are not exposed as proof.
- **Useful without private context** — a reader can follow the mechanism layer on its own terms.
- **Open to correction** — treated as revisable documentation, not sealed doctrine.

### Park

Material in the **park** is:

- **Valuable but not ready** — worth keeping, not yet shaped for public inspection.
- **Still forming** — edges, stakes, or wording may still shift.
- **Waiting for better wording, boundaries, or context** — needs digestion, cooling, or clearer framing before garden placement is honest.
- **Not deleted** — parked instead of discarded when the signal matters but the package does not.
- **Not automatically public** — visibility is deferred by default until routing criteria are met.

### Vault

Material in the **vault** is:

- **Raw, private, sensitive, identifiable, or externally impactful** — default-high-protection territory.
- **Protected by default** — treated as non-public input unless an explicit, careful decision says otherwise.
- **Not used as public evidence** — it does not justify public claims by mere existence or intensity.
- **Only abstracted into mechanisms after processing** — what crosses outward is pattern and boundary language, not a dump of source.

---

## Routing Questions

Before moving material toward the garden, ask:

- Does this expose a **person**, **private source**, or **raw event**?
- Is this a **mechanism** (repeatable pattern, guard, routing rule) or **only an intense moment**?
- Can this be **useful without** the private context?
- Is there **meaningful external impact** if this were published now?
- Is the **claim stronger than the evidence** available in public-safe form?
- Can this be expressed as **pattern**, **guard**, or **routing rule** instead of narrative pressure?

If answers lean toward exposure, intensity-only, or weak evidence, **park or vault** is usually more honest than garden.

---

## Movement Rules

- **Garden receives processed mechanism notes** — abstractions, protocols, navigation, and boundaries stated in public-safe language.
- **Park holds valuable but unfinished material** — preserve signal; defer publication until framing catches up.
- **Vault protects raw source material** — keep identifiable detail and high-impact raw inputs out of public mechanisms by default.
- **Movement from vault or park toward garden requires abstraction, claim-safety, and a human gate** — automation does not replace judgment about what may ship.
- **Deletion is not the first move; label before moving** — prefer routing and stripping over erasing when the underlying friction taught something.
- **Diff and check beat confidence** — small edits with visible deltas beat sweeping reassurance.
- **Smaller safe movements are preferred** — tighten wording, add guards, split scope before widening publicity.

---

## What This Prevents

- **Over-publication** — treating momentum as permission to broadcast.
- **Accidental exposure** — slipping identifiers or raw episodes into “general” notes.
- **AI over-confirmation** — letting fluent outputs harden claims without evidence-shaped boundaries.
- **Private source leakage** — using vault material as if it were already garden-ready.
- **Turning emotional intensity into public claim** — mistaking felt certainty for inspectable support.
- **Confusing mechanism with proof** — publishing a pattern as if it were verification of a specific lived arc.

---

## What This Enables

- **Faster safe publishing** when garden criteria are already met.
- **Clearer public navigation** — readers meet mechanisms, not background dossiers.
- **Better correction** — smaller, labeled moves are easier to revise.
- **Reusable patterns** — mechanisms compound across sessions without dragging raw context forward.
- **Protection of source while sharing mechanism** — the insight lane widens without forcing private material into the open.

---

## Status

This is a **working routing mechanism**. It may be corrected as the public field grows.
