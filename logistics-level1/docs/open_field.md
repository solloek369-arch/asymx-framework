# Open Field

AsymX Logistics Level 1 is intentionally open.

It is a static, local-only visibility lens for repeated warehouse deviations.
Everyone is invited to inspect, test, fork and improve it.

Please preserve the Level 1 spirit:
- no upload
- no backend
- no dependencies
- no tracking
- no real company data
- no hard claims without internal validation

The door is open.
The field stays clean.
