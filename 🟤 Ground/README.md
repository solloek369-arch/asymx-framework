# 🟤 Ground

**Aarde · Wortel · carrying**

Ground is where **signal touches weight**: root, body, soil, rhythm—whatever holds life **before** it becomes language.

## Status

**Motherflower layer** — substrate / support — not a proof layer.

## What belongs here

- Notes on **pace**, **carrying capacity**, **when to slow** before interpreting.
- Gentle anchors: “what needs to stay heavy enough to hold truth **without** crushing it?”
- Bridges toward [`⚫ Source`](../⚫%20Source/) (origin intact) and [`🟣 Mystery`](../🟣%20Mystery/) (depth without verdict).

## What does *not* belong here

- Hard claims about bodies, ecologies, or spirits as **authority**.
- Diagnostics, prescriptions, or “this soil means X” closure.

## Rule

> Ground answers first to **support**, not to **speed**.
