## Public source signal

Groenewout publishes an interview-format piece describing bol.com opening a **new returns facility (BRC)** in **Waalwijk**, opposite its fulfilment centre. Groenewout is presented as having supported **process design** (including defining success factors and visiting other returns operations). The BRC uses **partial mechanisation**: goods pass a **sorter** and move on **conveyors** to workstations where **trained operators** assess items and route them toward stock, discounted **return deals**, or onward routes described as **wholesale buyers or auctions**. The **stated aim** on the page is to give **virtually all returns a second life**. The page also cites **2020** revenue growth context (numeric figures appear **only** on that page — reproduce **only** from primary source when needed).
