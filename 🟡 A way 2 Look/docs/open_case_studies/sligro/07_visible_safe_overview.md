It contains no internal company data, performs no operational diagnosis, and makes no claims about internal software performance. See `00_ASYMX_PUBLIC_DATA_GOVERNANCE.md`.

## Purpose

Case 07 collects only the patterns from Case 01 through Case 06 that are visible enough, based on public sources, to review together.

It is a short overview layer. It does not turn public signals into conclusions. It keeps public-source visibility, AsymX interpretation, claim boundary and safe next step separated.
