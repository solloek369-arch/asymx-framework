---

## 0. Golden Rule

> Symbols in AsymX are not decoration.  
> They are routing logic.

A symbol does not prove anything.

A symbol does not create authority.

A symbol does not replace a source.

A symbol helps the field route what appears.

In AsymX, symbolic language is used to make movement lighter, clearer and more traceable.

Colors are routing states.

They are not decoration.

They tell the field what kind of handling is needed.

> Niet aankomen. Hier gist goud.

- unsorted material

## 4. Mycelium Architecture

The repository should not become a duplicated vault.

It should behave like a living mycelium.

The source may stay in one place.

Other places may point to it.

The pointer is often better than the copy.

---

### Principle

> No duplication when a pointer is enough.

---

### Stam / Tree

Function:

- central node
- trunk
- root relation
- place where one source can be held
