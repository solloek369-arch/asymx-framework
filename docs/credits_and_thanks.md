# Credits and Thanks

## Purpose

This document keeps a visible trace of people, tools, projects, sources and systems that helped AsymX become clearer.

It is not a marketing page.

It is a thank-you layer.

## Principle

When something helps, it leaves a trace.

When a person, tool or source makes movement possible, that movement can be acknowledged.

Thanks are given by naming what helped and what it made possible.
