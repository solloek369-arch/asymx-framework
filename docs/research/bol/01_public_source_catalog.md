## How to add or verify an entry

1. Open the page in a normal browser session.  
2. Copy the **canonical URL** (prefer stable site paths).  
3. Record **Retrieved on:** `YYYY-MM-DD`.  
4. In the matching case file, write summaries **only** from that visit (no numbers unless seen on page).

Long verbatim paste: avoid unless licence and citational practice allow; prefer short pointers plus careful summary checked against the live page.
