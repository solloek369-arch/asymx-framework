## What bol.com publicly appears to have solved or developed

**From `B1` (primary — newsroom highlights):**

- **Packaging / footprint (examples on page):** **2024:** **9%** of items shipped **without an outer box**, saving **4 million kg** cardboard; use of **recycled** cardboard mentioned.
- **Machinery:** conversion of packaging machines to **“Folding Green Machines”** described as reducing carbon emissions per package by **approximately 28%**; further conversions planned **2025**.
- **Parcel emissions:** overall **carbon emissions per package** described as down **over 5%** vs **2023**.
- **Repair / second life:** **159,000** broken products sent for repair in **2024**; **90,000** repaired and given a second life (remainder recycled — **as stated on `B1`**).
- **Refurbished electronics** proposition described (categories such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, smartwatches); **partner** quality framing including checks and **two-year warranty**; expansion plans **2025**.
- **Supplier climate:** **80%** of emissions attributed to **top 250 suppliers**; **Climate hub** (**“Klimaathub”**) described for **SBTi**-related targets/reporting; **2024:** largest suppliers shared emissions information (**all as on `B1`**).
- **Goede Keuze:** **1.5 million** products with that label on the platform in **2024**.
- **Social highlights** (examples on page): Bollebozen reach **800,000+** children/caregivers; **441,000+** books donated; inclusion metrics including **43.4%** women in leadership and **28%** women in Tech (**figures as printed on `B1`**).
- **Reporting:** **2024 Impact Report** described as aligning with **CSRD** standards (**as on `B1`**).
