# Open Meekijkwaaier

## Short Definition

The Open Meekijkwaaier is an open communication and collaboration pattern.

It opens a shared viewing space where people can look at the same visible material, correct it, complete it and route the next safe movement.

It is an Operationele Ontwapenings-interface.

## What It Is

- a short door into a shared meekijkruimte
- a way to show observations, examples, questions and patterns side by side
- a claim-safe structure for correction and co-ownership
- a visible route from uncertainty to the next safe movement
- a living communication layer, not a finished conclusion

## What It Is Not

- sales material
- consultancy method
- persuasion script
- finished method
- diagnosis
- audit
- proof
- solution claim
- company-specific output

## Why It Exists

People often learn faster when they can look at the same visible object together.

Open hypotheses and observations may invite expertise, correction and completion.

The goal is not to win a debate.

The goal is to make the shared field visible enough to review without extra pressure.

## Core Flow

Door -> meekijkruimte -> examples -> labels / claim status -> correction -> next safe movement

More visibility is allowed when claim status is clear.

The Open Meekijkwaaier keeps uncertainty open, labeled and reviewable.
