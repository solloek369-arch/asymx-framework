# Case 09 — Verification Invitations Map

## Purpose

This case does not claim an internal Sligro situation.

It collects public signals, general logistics patterns, and open uncertainty points that may be strong enough to serve as verification invitations.

A verification invitation is not evidence.
A verification invitation is a safe question that remains traceable to a public signal, a general pattern, or a clearly marked hypothesis.

Core rule:

> Wat zichtbaar genoeg is, hoeft nu niet opnieuw gedragen te worden.  
> Wat leeft, krijgt ruimte om te groeien.

## 3. Verification invitations

### 3.1 AS/400 → SAP as transfer lens

**Public signal:**  
Sligro’s transition from AS/400 legacy systems toward SAP is publicly visible.

**Why interesting:**  
Large ERP transitions often involve translation between older working logic, new standardized systems, masterdata, and daily execution.

**Why not claimable:**  
A public transition does not prove internal friction, failure, mismatch, or operational loss.

**Safe formulation:**  
“Because the AS/400 to SAP transition is publicly visible, it may be useful to verify which local knowledge, workarounds, or execution signals need to remain visible during standardization.”

**Route:**  
Case 06 / Case 07.

---

### 3.2 Masterdata migration as visibility lens

**Public signal:**  
Masterdata preparation appears in public SAP rollout context.

**Why interesting:**  
Masterdata is a core bridge between article data, location logic, replenishment, order-picking, and execution.

**Why not claimable:**  
A public masterdata migration context does not prove data quality issues.

**Safe formulation:**  
“Masterdata preparation is publicly visible as part of the transition. It can be treated as a neutral verification point for visibility between article data, location data, and execution signals.”

**Route:**  
Case 06.

---

### 3.3 PLOP / order-picking technology as process-quality lens

**Public signal:**  
Order-picking technology replacement / process-quality improvement is publicly mentioned.

**Why interesting:**  
Order-picking technology touches the exact interface between system instruction and floor action.

**Why not claimable:**  
The replacement of technology does not prove that the previous system caused problems.

**Safe formulation:**  
“Order-picking technology is a visible change layer. A small Level-1 lens could verify which execution signals become more visible before and after such a transition.”

**Route:**  
Case 07 / later validation.

---

### 3.4 Slim4 / replenishment versus real-time visibility lens

**Public signal:**  
Slim4 / replenishment / forecasting is publicly visible in relation to Sligro’s supply-chain context.

**Why interesting:**  
Forecasting predicts expected demand and replenishment, while AsymX Level 1 can focus on what becomes visible at the moment reality diverges from expectation.

**Why not claimable:**  
The existence of forecasting does not prove that forecasting is wrong or incomplete.

**Safe formulation:**  
“Forecasting and replenishment are publicly visible as planning layers. A complementary visibility question is whether local real-time deviation signals are visible early enough to reduce repeated human carrying.”

**Route:**  
Case 06 / Case 09 / later bol.com meta-case.

---

### 3.5 Phantom inventory / IRI as dummydata validation lens

**Public signal:**  
Inventory Record Inaccuracy, phantom inventory, ghost picks, stock discrepancies and short picks are known general logistics patterns.

**Why interesting:**  
They translate the abstract system-versus-floor question into small observable event patterns.

**Why not claimable:**  
General logistics literature is not evidence of a Sligro-specific situation.

**Safe formulation:**  
“Inventory Record Inaccuracy and phantom inventory are general logistics patterns. They can be used as dummydata validation lenses without claiming they occur inside Sligro.”

**Route:**  
Case 06.

## 5. Mail-safe language

### Safe as question

“Publieke bronnen laten zien dat meerdere systeem- en supply-chain lagen tegelijk bewegen. Zou een kleine, lokale zichtbaarheidslens behulpzaam kunnen zijn om te verifiëren welke signalen al zichtbaar genoeg zijn?”

### Safe as observation

“De publieke laag toont een context van ERP-transitie, masterdata-voorbereiding, supply-chain professionalisering, replenishment en order-picking technology change.”

### Safe as verification invitation

“Niet als conclusie, maar als verificatiepunt: welke kleine afwijkingssignalen zijn al zichtbaar genoeg om niet steeds opnieuw door mensen gedragen te hoeven worden?”

### Unsafe

Do not use:

- “Sligro heeft ghost picks.”
- “SAP veroorzaakt frictie.”
- “AS/400 was het probleem.”
- “De vloer repareert systeemfouten.”
- “Sligro faalt in voorraadbeheer.”
- “AsymX lost dit op.”
- “Dit bewijst operationele schade.”
