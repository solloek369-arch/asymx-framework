# Safety

Status: PUBLIC STRUCTURE

This folder contains public-safe AsymX safety mechanics.

Safety documents describe reusable protection structures, especially where human judgment, automation, external impact, or high-risk contexts require clearer gates.

The goal is not shame, punishment, or control.

The goal is earlier visibility, pre-committed protection, and safer action boundaries.

## Safety Principles

- visibility before damage
- protection before optimization
- human dignity stays intact
- high-impact contexts require stronger gates
- private triggers stay private
- public documents carry reusable mechanics only

## External-Impact Guards

External-impact guards are protection patterns for moments where a decision or action may affect other people, physical safety, public trust, money, systems, or irreversible outcomes.
