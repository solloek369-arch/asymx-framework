# Confirmation Path and the Role of AI

This note describes an AsymX working principle.

The system does not force truth too early.

Everything begins as signal, pattern, assumption, hypothesis or visible movement.

A statement becomes stronger only through traceable confirmation.

## Core rule

AI does not determine truth.

AI organizes, structures and guards the confirmation path.

## Confirmation path

AsymX treats knowledge as a path.

1. Signal  
   Something appears.

2. Pattern  
   Something appears more than once or connects to other movements.

3. Hypothesis  
   A pattern becomes stable enough to examine.

4. First confirmation  
   One person or source confirms that the pattern is recognizable.

5. Group confirmation  
   A wider group confirms that the pattern works for shared observation.

6. Deviation round  
   Any non-confirmation, exception or disagreement is captured as information.

7. Correction  
   The pattern is refined without deleting the trace.

8. Wider confirmation  
   The refined pattern is tested with a wider circle.

9. Shared working fact  
   The pattern is confirmed enough to guide shared work, while remaining open to correction.

10. Universal confirmation  
   Only when all affected humans have confirmed the path can the system approach full fact-status.

In practice, most work happens before universal confirmation.

That is not failure.

That is the normal learning field.

## Why this matters

When a system calls something fact too early, it can close observation.

When a system keeps something visible as a pattern on a confirmation path, it can still learn.

The question is not only:

> is this true?

The better questions are:

- what is visible?
- who has confirmed it?
- who has not confirmed it?
- what deviation appeared?
- what changed after correction?
- what circle is ready for the next confirmation?
- what level is this statement allowed to occupy now?

## Deviation as information

A deviation is not an attack on the path.

A deviation is information.

A non-confirmation is not failure.

A non-confirmation shows where the path is not complete yet.

The system does not remove the pattern.

The system marks the level and keeps learning.

## Role of AI

AI is not the owner of truth.

AI is not the judge of final reality.

AI can help by:

- collecting signals;
- grouping patterns;
- preserving traces;
- separating claims from sources;
- showing uncertainty;
- naming the current confirmation level;
- keeping correction possible;
- recording who or what confirmed a step;
- recording where the pattern did not confirm;
- preparing the next confirmation round.

## Closing rule

Do not force a pattern into a fact too early.

Do not throw away a pattern because it is not yet a fact.

Let the level be visible.

Let the confirmation path remain open.

Let every deviation become information.
