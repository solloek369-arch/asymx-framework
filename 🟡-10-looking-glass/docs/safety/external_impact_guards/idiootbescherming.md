# Idiootbescherming

Subtitle: Always-On Field Guard / External-Impact Guard / Pre-Commit Safety Gate

Status: PUBLIC SAFETY ANCHOR

## Core Meaning

Idiootbescherming is the official AsymX awareness name for a protection gate that activates when in-the-moment judgment may become unreliable and external impact may become unsafe.

The name is intentionally direct.

It is not used as shame, punishment, or insult.

It is used as a fast awareness trigger: a moment where protection is needed before negotiation begins.

## Why This Exists

Some contexts can temporarily weaken judgment, increase impulsivity, or make a person overconfident about risk.

In those moments, relying on in-the-moment feeling may not be safe enough.

Idiootbescherming means that a sober, earlier, clearer rule protects the later moment when judgment may be less reliable.

## Core Rule

When a high-risk context appears, the risky option is removed before the impaired or impulsive state can negotiate with it.

The system does not need to prove a person is impaired.

The system detects a high-risk context and activates a pre-committed protection gate.

## Example Pattern

Risk context:

- impaired-state possibility
- vehicle access
- impulsive mood
- external impact potential

Guard response:

- no driving decision in the moment
- no major irreversible action
- safe alternatives only
- social or physical gate when needed
- later trace without shame

## Watch / Always-On Field Direction

A smartwatch can act as a direct awareness surface because it stays close to the body.

A minimal watch guard may include:

- one-tap activation
- red-mode signal
- strong haptic warning
- safe alternatives
- trusted-contact option
- next-morning trace

The watch does not guarantee safety.

It supports a pre-committed protection rule and reduces reliance on unreliable in-the-moment judgment.

## Naming Rule

The primary awareness name remains:

Idiootbescherming

Other terms may be used as subtitles or translations:

- Always-On Field Guard
- Always-On Watch Guard
- External-Impact Guard
- Pre-Commit Safety Gate
- Impaired-State Protection

These terms help explain the mechanism, but they do not replace the awareness name.

## Public / Private Boundary

Public documentation may describe the mechanism.

Private incidents, names, locations, raw emotional traces, or identifiable details belong in the local vault.
