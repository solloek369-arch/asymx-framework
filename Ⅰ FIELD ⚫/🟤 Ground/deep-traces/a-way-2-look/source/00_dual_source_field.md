## Claim-Safety & Cultural Respect

The inclusion of Quechua/Kichwa is an architectural origin reflection, not a claim of complete linguistic authority, cultural ownership, or representation of all Quechua/Kichwa varieties.

The Eagle and Condor prophecy is treated as a pattern mirror, not as proof, mandate or ownership claim.

The repository can carry this pattern respectfully by making the relation visible, open, traceable and correctable.

## Translation Governance

The source/nl/ directory carries exact Dutch source wording.
The source/qu/ directory carries Quechua/Kichwa origin reflections and translations when possible.
The source/qu/ layer does not assume complete authority, ownership, perfect one-to-one translation or representation of all Quechua/Kichwa varieties.
Every Quechua/Kichwa reflection starts as:
OPEN / NEEDS NATIVE CHECK

A reflection can move toward reviewed or grounded status only when it has been checked by native, community-grounded or otherwise authorized linguistic knowledge.
