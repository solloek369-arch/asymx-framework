# Tentacle Index

This index separates the AsymX core mechanism from the fields it may invite.

AsymX is not one application.
AsymX is not a content factory.
AsymX is not a domain claim.

AsymX is an open invitation mechanism.

The core mechanic remains:

> public trace → question-first lens → source-jump → open invitation

A tentacle is not the system.
A tentacle is a temporary landing field where the mechanism may become useful.

## Separation Rule

Always separate:

1. **Core Mechanic**  
   The stable AsymX mechanism.

2. **Tentacle**  
   The field where the mechanism temporarily lands.

3. **Own Idea**  
   A possible interpretation, proposal, or creative direction.

4. **Invited Field**  
   A domain that may recognize itself in the mechanism.

5. **Source Trail**  
   Public traces that can be placed in relation through a question-first lens.
