# Level 1 Logic — Story Filter

This lens receives pasted text and keeps the original source intact.

It lowers pressure by giving incoming text one of three first-pass routes:

- VERHAALOPBOUW: material that builds the story or structure
- DUBBEL / HERHALING: repeated material that may be compressed later
- RUIS / NU NIET: material that can be parked out deletion

The lens does not decide final truth.
It does not delete source.
It only makes the first split visible.
