# 🟠 Flow

**Resultaat · Vrucht · zichtbare beweging**

Flow is **what becomes visible after something moves**: fruit, outcome sketch, “this is what shifted”—without locking the whole field to one metric.

## Status

**Motherflower layer** — outcome visibility — **temporary by default**.

## What belongs here

- Short **after-move notes**: what changed, what stayed ambiguous, what can rest.
- Bridges to [`⚪ The Web`](../⚪%20The%20Web/) when traces deserve preservation **without** becoming a campaign.
- Hooks back to [`↺ terug naar Source`](../↺%20terug%20naar%20Source/) when the loop should compost rather than stack.

## What does *not* belong here

- ROI certainty, blame narratives, or “final truth” language.

## Rule

> Flow shows **movement**, not **dominion**.

---

*Good flow ends without stealing oxygen from the next cycle.*
